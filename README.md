# 🌍 Earthian AI

### AI-Powered Travel Assistant for Intelligent Travel Planning

Earthian AI is an AI-powered travel assistant designed to help users discover destinations, plan trips, explore local experiences, and generate personalized travel recommendations through natural language interaction.

This project was developed as a final project for the **Maju Bareng AI for Data Scientist Program**, organized by **Hacktiv8**.

Earthian AI combines **Generative AI, Python, Streamlit, and the Google Gemini API** to create an interactive conversational travel assistant that helps users discover destinations and plan their next journey.

---

## ✨ Project Overview

Planning a trip can often be time-consuming.

Travelers may need to search across multiple platforms to find:

- Suitable destinations
- Travel itineraries
- Popular attractions
- Hidden gems
- Local food recommendations
- Transportation options
- Accommodation suggestions
- Estimated travel budgets
- Practical travel tips

Earthian AI aims to simplify this process by providing a centralized AI-powered travel assistant.

Instead of searching across multiple platforms, users can interact with the assistant using natural language.

For example:

> "Recommend a destination in Indonesia for my next vacation."

or:

> "Create a realistic 3-day itinerary for Bali."

Earthian AI then generates a conversational response based on the user's travel needs and preferences.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The main objective of Earthian AI is to develop an interactive AI travel assistant that helps users:

1. Discover travel destinations.
2. Plan personalized trips.
3. Generate travel itineraries.
4. Explore popular attractions and hidden gems.
5. Discover local food experiences.
6. Explore transportation and accommodation options.
7. Understand estimated travel budgets.
8. Receive practical travel tips.
9. Interact with an AI assistant through natural language.

This project also demonstrates the practical implementation of **Generative AI in the travel and tourism industry**.

---

## 🧠 Why Earthian AI?

The name **Earthian AI** represents an intelligent assistant that helps people explore the Earth.

The concept combines:

- **Earth** → The planet, destinations, cultures, and travel experiences.
- **-ian** → A connection to people, exploration, and human experiences.
- **AI** → Artificial Intelligence as the technology behind the assistant.

Earthian AI is designed as a digital travel companion that helps users discover new places and plan meaningful journeys.
